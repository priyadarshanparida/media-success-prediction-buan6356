## Media Success Prediction: Using Billboard Rank, Streaming Analytics, Audio Features, & Social Buzz

1. Dataset:
   [Media Success Prediction (Billboard Top 100)](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/priyadarshanparida/media-success-prediction-billboard-top-100)
2. Focal Dependent Variable: Binary variable “HIT” indicating whether the song was a hit (1) or not (0).
3. Motivation Behind the Analytics: The success prediction of a song can be used to inform decisions across pre-production and post-production of audio tracks. Streaming platforms, record labels, and media firms could utilize these predictions for Artist and Repertoire (A&R) decisions, timing marketing actions, and ROI-based investment in artists.
